# Your Life is an A4 Sheet of Paper 📄

> 75 years. 900 squares. 1 square = 1 month of your life.

**[→ Try it live](https://ordinarymantrying.com/tools/life-a4/)**

---

## What is this?

A single-page interactive tool that visualizes your entire life on one A4 sheet of paper.

Each of the **900 squares** represents one month. The grid is 30×30 — clean, complete, confronting.

- ⬛ **Dark squares** = months already lived
- 🟥 **Red square** = right now
- ⬜ **Light squares** = months remaining
- 🟩 **Green squares** = bonus months unlocked by healthy habits (up to 110 years)

---

## Features

### 10 Life Scenarios (before you enter your birth month)
See what different life chapters look like on the grid — before the tool shows yours:

- 🧒 Age 20 — 240 squares already gone
- 🎓 College graduate — 48 squares for university
- 💕 A 6-month romance — just 6 squares on a blank grid
- 💼 Age 30 grind — 360 squares behind you
- 👶 New parent (before kindergarten) — 36 squares with your baby
- 🏫 Raising a child (age 6–18) — 144 squares under one roof
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Your parents at 50 — 600 squares already gone, 300 left
- 📅 See them every day — all 300 remaining squares together
- 📆 Twice a month — only 20 out of 300
- 🗓️ Once a year — **just 4 squares** out of 300. Four.

### Your Personal Grid
Enter your birth month → see exactly where you are on the paper.

### 7 Longevity Habits
Drag sliders to unlock up to 420 bonus months (35 extra years). Based on research from *Outlive* by Peter Attia.

### Seal Your Wishes
Write 1–3 wishes on your A4 paper. They appear written on the grid and print with it.

### Download & Print
- **Download PNG** — shareable image with your grid, wishes, habits, and QR code
- **Print & Hang on Wall** — A4-optimized print with daily habit checkboxes
- **Save scenario images** — each of the 10 scenarios generates a shareable image with date and QR code

### Late Bloomer Dots 🔴
Red dots mark the exact month when famous people had their greatest breakthrough. Hover to see who — most of them started *after* you.

| Age | Who | What |

| 40 | Vera Wang | Left figure skating, began designing wedding dresses |

| 65 | Colonel Sanders | Started franchising KFC |

## Tech Stack

- **Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS** — zero frameworks, zero dependencies (besides two CDN libs)
- [html2canvas](https://html2canvas.hertzen.com/) — for PNG export
- [QRCode.js](https://github.com/soldair/node-qrcode) — for QR codes in exported images
- PHP backend (optional) — community wishes wall with 5-layer spam defense

Single file. No build step. No login. No data collected.
